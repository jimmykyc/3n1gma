Introduction to The Enigma

The fire within my lonely soul fades away, slowly dissipating into the dust. I grasped the destructive power within my burning hand and ventured past the ominous black mountain.

In the mist, an echo resounds, and I find solace alongside my shadow beneath the soothing glow of the moonlight. The moon's melody pierces the darkness like a whispered tune, and I surrender to the ruler of the midnight realm. Below the vast emptiness, I encounter my alternate self in this ethereal dimension. There exists a profound sense of connection, as if bound by mystical forces, and an enigmatic longing to uncover the unseen.

The cypress path mirrors my own journey, reflected within the scrying glass of my soul.

Burn, burn, burn. Smoke from a million dusk come my way. Running through the halls of that eternal ending.

Hand in hand with the eye that molds me.
